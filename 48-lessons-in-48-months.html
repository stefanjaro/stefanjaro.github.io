Its definition is self-explanatory. Its purpose, however, is powerful.

Never move on from anything --- a project, an analysis, a report, a conversation --- without spending some time reflecting on what you\'ve learned.

After all, knowledge, like interest, compounds over time.

In the spirit of the term, here are the 48 lessons I\'ve learned from my leaders, colleagues, and clients over the 48 months I spent at Stax, a global strategy consulting firm.
